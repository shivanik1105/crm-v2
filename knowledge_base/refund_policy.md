# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Rules

### 14-Day Window
No refunds are issued after 14 days from the payment date. This is a hard rule with no exceptions for standard cases.

### Eligibility
Refunds within 14 days are processed automatically for:
- Duplicate charges
- Billing errors caused by our system
- Accidental plan upgrades (if no usage of new features)

## Exception Process

For refund requests outside the 14-day window:
1. Submit detailed request to billing@company.com
2. Include reason for refund, account details, and supporting documentation
3. Requests reviewed within 5 business days by Billing Review Committee
4. Approval requires VP Finance sign-off for amounts over $1,000
5. If approved, refund processed within 10 business days to original payment method

## Credits vs Refunds

### Preferred: Account Credits
For service-related issues, we prefer issuing account credits rather than refunds. Credits:
- Applied immediately to account
- Never expire
- Can be used for any future invoice
- Full flexibility for customer

### Refunds
Refunds are reserved for:
- Billing errors
- Duplicate charges
- Cases where customer is closing account permanently

## Retention Playbook

### Churn Threat Response
For accounts with monthly value >$500/month threatening to churn:
1. Immediate escalation to Account Executive + VP Customer Success
2. Offer: 2-month service credit as retention incentive
3. Schedule executive call within 48 hours
4. Document churn reason for Product team

### Public Review Threats
If customer threatens public negative review:
- Do not offer refund unless within 14-day window
- Offer 2-month credit on Standard tier ($298 value)
- Escalate to Marketing + CEO if review already published
- Document interaction for legal review if defamatory

**Karen Scenario:** Karen is on Standard monthly ($149/mo) and threatened a public review. Her account value is below the $500 threshold, so the standard retention offer is a 2-month credit ($298 value). No refund is offered since she is outside the 14-day window.

## Chargeback Policy
Customers who initiate chargebacks without contacting support first may have their accounts suspended pending review. We vigorously dispute fraudulent chargebacks.
