# Pricing Policy

## Subscription Tiers

### Starter
- **Price:** $49 per month
- **Users:** Up to 5 users
- **Features:** Core CRM features, email integration, basic reporting
- **Support:** Email support only, 48-hour response SLA

### Standard
- **Price:** $149 per month
- **Users:** Up to 25 users
- **Features:** Advanced automation, custom pipelines, API access, webhook support
- **Support:** Priority email + chat support, 24-hour response SLA
- **Annual discount:** 20% when paid yearly ($1,430.40/year)

### Enterprise
- **Price:** Custom pricing (typically $2,000-$5,000+/mo)
- **Users:** Unlimited
- **Features:** Dedicated infrastructure, SSO/SAML, advanced security, HIPAA BAA available
- **Support:** Dedicated Customer Success Manager, 4-hour response SLA, phone support

## Billing Rules

### Pro-Rata Billing
When a customer upgrades mid-cycle, they are charged pro-rata from the upgrade date. The first invoice after upgrade reflects a partial month. The billing anniversary resets to the upgrade date.

**Example:** Alice Smith is on Standard annual ($1,430.40/year). She upgrades to Enterprise on the 15th of the month. Her next invoice will be: (Enterprise monthly price * remaining days in month / total days in month). Her annual billing anniversary becomes the 15th.

### Annual Plans
- 20% discount applied to equivalent monthly price
- Non-refundable after 30-day satisfaction window
- Customer may switch to monthly at any time, but loses annual discount retroactively
- Downgrades from annual to monthly require billing team approval

### Non-Profit Discount
- 30% off Standard tier only
- Requires valid 501(c)(3) documentation
- Does not apply to Starter or Enterprise tiers
- Must be renewed annually with updated documentation
- Cannot be combined with annual discount (larger discount takes precedence)

## Plan Changes

### Upgrades
- Immediate upon request
- Pro-rata charge for remainder of current billing period
- New features available immediately

### Downgrades
- Effective at end of current billing period
- No partial refunds for unused time
- Customer notified 7 days before downgrade takes effect

## Payment Methods
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Invoice billing (Enterprise only, Net-30 terms)
